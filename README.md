# Scratch-Off Simulator

A simple python scratch-off game for the terminal.

You start with $100. Pick a card, buy one or more, and scratch them to see what
you win. Every card has its own price and jackpot — some scratches hit big, most
don't. Grow your wallet and cash out while you're ahead, or keep pulling until
you go broke.

## How to play

Run `python3 game.py`.

Pick a card by its ID, choose how many to buy, then watch the scratch. After each
round you can cash out or keep going.
